Peter Smith is James Smith's father and he wants to be able to track James' grade. In order for Peter to achive this, you, as the admin of Moodle needs to help him. Follow these simple steps to make sure Peter gets his neccessary permission, no more, no less.

1. Make sure that Peter is registered as a Moodle user
2. Go to James' profile


3. Click on Assign role relative to this user on the right panel.


4. Click parent.


5. Search for Peter's name, then click Add. There's no submit or confirm button so once you've clicked on the Add button you're done!
